Welcome to WWW.edu!

World Wide Women’s Education

“Education is education. We should learn everything and then choose which path to follow. Education is neither Eastern nor Western, it is human.” –Malala Yousafzai

WWW-edu Purpose

The dominant problem that www.edu is trying to solve is the inequality of education around the world, and we also wanted to focus on the gender gap in education to connect our topic to Kode With Klossy.

What this site includes:

This site includes charts contrasting the education levels with data taken from 6 countries: 2 emerging, 2 developing, and 2 developed countries. This site also includes resources and an about us page with information on the creators.
